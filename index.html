About Karina

A neighbor before an agent.

I’m Karina Taveras, a real estate professional with Coldwell Banker Realty’s Southwest Broward office. I help buyers and sellers across Miramar, Pembroke Pines, Weston, and the surrounding South Broward neighborhoods navigate one of the most personal decisions they’ll ever make.

How I Help

Specialties built for real life.

Every transaction is different. These are the four areas where I spend most of my time — and where my experience can make the biggest difference for you.

First-Time Buyers

Your first home, without the guesswork.

From pre-approval to closing day, I walk first-time buyers through every step in plain language, both English and Spanish. No surprises, no jargon — just the answers you need to feel confident.

Family Homes

Schools, space, and staying put.

South Broward families know what matters: the right school zone, the right backyard, the right block. I help growing families find homes they can settle into for the long run.

Sellers & Listings

Position your home to move.

Pricing strategy, professional marketing, and the network of a global brokerage behind every listing. The goal is simple: the strongest possible offer in the shortest reasonable time.

Relocation

New to South Florida? Let’s start there.

Moving to Broward from out of state — or from another country — comes with its own set of questions. I help newcomers find their footing, their neighborhood, and their first home down here.
